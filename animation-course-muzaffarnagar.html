Looking for an animation institute near you?

Mosaic Works Institute offers classroom-based animation training at Mahaveer Chowk, Muzaffarnagar, with project work, mentor feedback and portfolio guidance.

This course is designed for beginners, students after 12th and creative learners who want to understand movement, timing, composition and storytelling. You build work that can be shown to studios, agencies and clients.

What you learn

  • Animation principles and storyboarding
  • 2D motion and character basics
  • 3D modelling, lighting and animation foundations
  • Showreel and portfolio development
  • Freelance and interview preparation

Practical learning path

01 · Foundations

Design, composition, timing, spacing, colour and visual storytelling.

02 · Production

Guided exercises and project workflows from idea to final output.

03 · Portfolio

Selected projects refined into a focused animation showreel.

Animation course FAQs

Can a complete beginner join?

Yes. Training begins with visual and animation fundamentals before moving into production projects.

Is this suitable after 12th?

Yes. Students after 12th can learn animation as a portfolio-led creative career skill. Course choice should match the student's goals and available study time.

Do you provide placement support?

The institute provides portfolio, interview and opportunity guidance. Employment depends on skill level, portfolio quality and available openings; no job outcome is guaranteed.
