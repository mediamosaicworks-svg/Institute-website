Motion graphics course FAQs

Is motion graphics the same as animation?

They overlap, but motion graphics commonly focuses on animated type, shapes, branding and information, while animation is a broader field that can include characters and narrative scenes.

Do I need design experience?

No. Beginners can start with fundamentals. Existing graphic design or editing experience can help you progress faster.

Which software is taught?

After Effects is central to the course. Supporting tools and workflows may vary by project; ask during counselling for the current syllabus.
