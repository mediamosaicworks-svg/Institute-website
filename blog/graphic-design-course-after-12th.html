Graphic Design Course After 12th

Students from arts, commerce or science can learn graphic design. A strong course develops visual thinking, software control and a portfolio—not only a certificate.

What should beginners learn?

Start with typography, colour, layout, composition and image selection. Then apply these principles through Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW or Canva projects.

Certificate or portfolio?

A certificate records course completion. A portfolio demonstrates what you can do. Understand the certificate terms while giving serious attention to polished projects.

Career and freelancing preparation

Beginner paths can include junior graphic designer, social-media designer or production designer. Freelancers also need client communication, pricing, revisions and file-delivery skills. Income or employment is never guaranteed by a course alone.

How to choose an institute

Meet the mentor, see recent student portfolios, understand the full fee and ask how projects receive feedback. Avoid choosing through salary promises alone.
